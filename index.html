Promyc® as an Industrial Ingredient

Promyc® is a vegan mycoprotein ingredient produced through biotechnology with the fermentation process as a core. Our production process allows us to implement a lot more time- ,
and cost- and feedstock-efficient than traditional farming, with much less environmental impact. It also enables the decentralization of food production, making it possible to produce Promyc® wherever needed.

Promyc Fat

The Promyc fat consists of a fungi-stabilized emulsion gel where the fat achieves a behaviour very similar to structured meat fat in both melting ability and taste aspects.

You can use the Promyc fat in a wide range of products. It obtains a controlled fat release and can therefore attain a prolonged mouthfeel in taste and richness resulting in juicier plant-based products.

What Can You Make with Promyc Fat?

Promyc fat can be used in many different products. From burgers and meatballs to achieve a marbled structure, to emulsified products such as sausages or high moisture extruded meat-like structures. It is also possible to use it in 3D printing, creating marbled whole cut. Basically, all type of animal meat analogues can be achieved.

Promyc® as a Semi-finished Product

Besides a sustainable ingredient, Promyc® also comes in a wide range of delicious ready-to-cook products. Nuggets, Crispy Burger, Vego Balls, and Schnitzel all made from Promyc® with the ability to offer ready-to-cook products to finally be ready to eat.



Crispy Burger

A true favourite! With its firm texture and its juiciness, the crispy burger is your ultimate vegan and gluten-free option for breaded chicken. Since optimally prepared directly from the freezer, your sustainable comfort food is just minutes away!

Tôna Flakes

Our Tôna flakes have a fishy and fibrous texture, perfect for salads, creamy toppings or simply serve as it is. We strongly recommend trying it out as a layer in your Smörgåstårta - we have tried it, and its delicious!

Nuggets

Delicious golden nuggets, coated in crispy gluten free breading and with a chewy bite like that of chicken. Convinced you would pass a blind test? Find out by trying them!

Vego Balls

Savoury Promyc® balls, seasoned with allspice and nutmeg to offer you the vegan version of the Swedish “Husmanskost” . Best served with potatoes, cream sauce, and lingonberries.

Schnitzel

With a bite and the taste of a classic veal schnitzel, our sustainable Promyc-based option is covered in gluten-free breading and is suitable for vegans. Whether you want to serve it with classic toppings, like capers and lemon, or slice it up Japanese katsu style is entirely up to you. !
